The Evidence of Coverage (EOC) booklet tells you how to get your Medicare medical care and prescription drugs covered through our plan. This booklet explains your rights and responsibilities, what is covered, and what you pay as a member of the plan. Chapter 4 of the EOC focuses on your covered services and what you pay for your medical benefits. It includes a Medical Benefits Chart that lists your covered services and shows how much you will pay for each covered service as a member of Ultimate Health Plans. It also has information about medical services that are not covered and explains limits on certainÂ services.
